About Us

The Blood Availability And Donor Management System is a comprehensive platform designed to manage Blood Donors.

Blood Availability and Donor Management System is a browser-based system that is designed to store, process, retrieve the information of registerd donors.

With the website, hospitals and required people can:

  • Track available blood donors within the location
  • Maintain a database of blood donors
