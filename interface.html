The Need for Blood

Blood is essential for various medical procedures and saves countless lives.
Blood is important because it performs several crucial functions in the body
Supplies oxygen to tissues
Carries nutrients such as glucose, amino acids, and fatty acids
Helps the immune system defend against intruders
Manages bleeding when injured

Blood Tips

Drink plenty of water before donating blood to stay hydrated.
Get plenty of sleep the night before.
Eat a healthy meal, avoiding fatty foods.
Drink plenty of water.
Check if any medications would prevent you from donating.

Who You Could Help

Many patients rely on blood donors for their treatment and recovery.
Donating blood can help patients of all ages, including accident and burn victims, heart surgery and organ transplant patients, and those battling cancer
